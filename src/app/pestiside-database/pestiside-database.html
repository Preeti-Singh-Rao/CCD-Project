Indian Pesticide Database

IPD which offers pesticide product registration/approval information.
  • Product Registered under Section 9(3), Section 9(3B), Section 9(4)
  • Registration information of bio-pesticides
  • Registration information for Export of existing/new Technical and formulations
  • Endorsement information for transfer of Certificate of Registration / address change / company name change
  • Inclusion information of new molecule/chemical in schedule of Insecticides Act, 1968
